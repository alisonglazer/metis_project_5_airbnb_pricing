About this tool.

Use this tool to calculate the suggested price for an Airbnb listing for a given day. The model used to generate base price was trained on listings in Los Angeles, CA with a high occupancy rate, such that the suggested prices should yield high booking rates as well. Time series analysis yields suggested price changes as functions of day of week, time of year, and holidays. You will also see 5 listings similar to the searched features, all of which have high occupancy rates, for hosts to compare their own listings.
